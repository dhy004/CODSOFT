50 years of serving communitioes

Our story begins in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle' s historic place Market.It was here where Starbucks opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from the world for our customers to take home Our name was inspired by theclassic tale,"Moby-Dick," evoking the sefaring tradition of the early coffee traders. Ten years later,a young New yorker named Howard Schultz would walk throgh these doors and become captivated with Starbucks Coffee from his first sip. After joining the company in 1982, a different cobblestone road would lead him another discovery. It was on a trip to Milan in 1983 that Howard first experienced Italy's coffeehouses' and he returned to seattle to bring the warmth and artistry of its coffee culture to Starbucks.By 1987, we swapped our browns for green ones and embarked on our next chapter as a coffeehouse.
